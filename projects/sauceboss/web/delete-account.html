Delete your SauceBoss account

Two paths — pick whichever applies.
← Back to SauceBoss

If you can sign in

The fastest path is to delete your account from inside the app:

  • Open SauceBoss on your phone or in the web app.
  • Sign in.
  • Tap the avatar pill in the top-right of the home screen → Settings.
  • Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete account.
  • Confirm. Your profile and all your favorites are removed immediately.

If you can't sign in

Email us from the address tied to the account and we'll delete it manually, normally within 30 days (typically the same day).

Email a deletion request

Email: privacy@sauceboss.app

What gets deleted

  • Your profile row (display name, admin flag, sign-in metadata).
  • Every favorite you've saved.
  • Your Supabase Auth identity (so you can't sign back in with the same address without creating a new account).

What stays

  • Recipes you authored stay in the public catalog so they don't disappear from other users mid-meal — but they lose their owner attribution and become unowned. Only an admin can edit or delete them after that.
  • If you'd prefer your authored recipes deleted as well, mention that in the deletion request email and we'll handle it manually.
  • Database backups — Supabase keeps automated backups for 7 days. Your data is purged from those backups within 7 days of account deletion as the backups roll over.

Questions?
